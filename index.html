Blender Conference 2023
Amsterdam, Netherlands · October 26–28, 2023
Celebrated 21 years of open-source Blender. Key topics: Simulation Zones in Geometry Nodes, Grease Pencil evolution, Flamenco render farm, glTF ecosystem, USD pipeline integration, and a first look at what would become Blender 4.x.

Blender Conference 2022
Amsterdam, Netherlands · October 27–29, 2022
BCON's triumphant return after two pandemic years (2020 & 2021 were cancelled). The largest BCON ever at the time, with 700+ attendees and 94 recorded presentations. Marked Blender's emergence as a serious industry tool.

BCON 2020 & 2021
Cancelled — COVID-19 pandemic
Both editions were cancelled due to the global pandemic. The Blender community held online events and developer streams in their place. BCON returned in October 2022.

ℹ️ ABOUT THIS ARCHIVE
🎯
What this is
Curated field guides for every Blender Conference. Each page summarizes talks, links to YouTube recordings, and provides context — so you know what to watch and why before you press play.
🌐
Open on GitHub
This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. It's open-source, static HTML with no tracking. You can fork it, contribute, or self-host. See the README for details.
📺
All talks are free
Every talk from every BCON is freely available on the official Blender YouTube channel (or CG Cookie for BCON LA). All links in these guides go directly to the source.
🧑‍💻
Who it's for
Built for the full spectrum: hobbyist discovering Blender, freelancer evaluating tools, studio professional benchmarking pipelines. Talks are tagged and described for all levels.
